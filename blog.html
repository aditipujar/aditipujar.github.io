The Belousov Zhabotinsky Reaction

The BZ reaction is a chemical oscillator that exhibits periodic changes in color due to the oscillation of reactant concentrations. Extensively studied for its complex behavior, including pattern formation and chaos, we recreated it in the laboratory to apply our analysis pipelines to real world time-series data.
